### 📌 Featured Research & Projects

* 🎓 **[NERVA: Nexus for Enterprise Risk and Vulnerability Assessment](LINK_ALLA_TUA_REPO)** *My Master's Degree Thesis and the core project of my Purple Team internship. I built a contextual AI pipeline (Flask + Llama 3.1 via GROQ API) designed to automate the parsing of security reports and map them to enterprise frameworks.*
  
  *Instead of just asking an LLM to "find bugs", NERVA is engineered to process vulnerabilities hierarchically:*
  1. **Context & Tech:** Understands the affected asset.
  2. **Vulnerability & Exploit:** Analyzes the technical flaw and its exploitation path.
  3. **Tactic Mapping:** Correlates the exploit to specific MITRE ATT&CK tactics.
  4. **Actionable Remediation:** Suggests defensive countermeasures based on MITRE D3FEND and maps the compliance impact on NIST CSF 2.0.

  *🔥 **The real deal:** It features a Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) validation system. The AI does the heavy lifting, but the security analyst reviews, corrects, and refines the mappings, creating a continuous feedback loop that improves the system's accuracy over time.*
